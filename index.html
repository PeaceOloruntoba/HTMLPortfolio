About Me

Hello! I’m Peace Oloruntoba.

I am not your normal developer

I am a problem solver and analytical thinker, able to troubleshoot and debug software issues with ease. I am also a team player and a good communicator able to work effectively with both technical and non-technical stakeholders

I am well-versed in various software development methodologies, including Agile and Scrum, and have experience working in both small teams and large-scale projects.
